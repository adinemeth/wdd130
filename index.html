Hungary

Hungary, located in Central Europe, is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and stunning architecture. The capital, Budapest, is famous for its thermal baths, the Parliament building, and the Chain Bridge. Hungary is also known for its delicious cuisine, including goulash and chimney cake, and its deep-rooted traditions in music and folk dance.
